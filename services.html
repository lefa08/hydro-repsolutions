What We Do

QUARTERLY HYDRAULIC SERVICES

At Africa Hydro Rep Solutions, we provide professional hydraulic and load body services to keep your vehicles operating at peak performance. Our quarterly maintenance services are designed to ensure the integrity and reliability of your hydraulic and load body systems, minimizing downtime and maximizing safety.

Our team of experienced technicians conducts thorough inspections, diagnostics, and repairs to address any issues proactively. With our expertise, you can trust your vehicles to remain safe, efficient, and fully operational.


Our comprehensive hydraulic services include:

  • Filter Replacement: Replacing all hydraulic filters for optimal performance.

  • Suction Strainer Cleaning: Ensuring a clean and efficient hydraulic system.

  • Grease Nipples Inspection: Checking and replacing grease nipples as needed.

  • Hydraulic Oil Preparation and Pressure Resetting: Ensuring proper hydraulic function with detailed system checks and reports.

  • Hydraulic Cylinder Inspections: Examining and repairing components as required.

  • Mounting Clip Maintenance: Checking for loose clips and addressing issues.

  • Pin and Bush Inspection: Identifying excessive wear to maintain system durability.

  • Guide and Slide Maintenance: Inspecting for wear to ensure smooth operation.

  • PTO and Pump Checks: Detecting potential damage or leaks to prevent failures.

  • Load Body Inspection: Assessing for cracks or other structural damages.

  • Tailgate and Hopper Maintenance: Inspecting the tailgate, hopper floor, and sweeper blade for wear and tear.

  • Hose Examination: Checking all hoses for signs of wear or potential failure.

WEEKLY VEHICLE FLEET SERVICES

Our weekly fleet inspections are designed to ensure your vehicles operate efficiently, safely, and reliably. During each inspection, we thoroughly assess your equipment and provide professional recommendations for areas requiring attention.

What We Check:

  • Hydraulic Cylinders: Inspect for leaks and ram damage.

  • Hydraulic Piping: Check for leaks and loose mounting clips.

  • Cycle Times:Measure to ensure optimal operational performance.

  • Stabilizers: Assess the operation of stabilizers on cherry pickers and truck-mounted cranes.

  • Cherry Picker Structure and Safety: Inspect structural integrity and verify safety certificate validity.

  • Cables and Ropes:Check for wear and tear to ensure safety and functionality.

  • Truck-Mounted Crane StabilityInspect for structural issues and verify the health of certification.

  • Lubrication: Inspect and grease the rooster sheave, jib sheaves, and jib winches for smooth operation.

Comprehensive Reporting

After each inspection, we provide a detailed report highlighting:

  • Any damage, misuse, or abuse of equipment.
  • Repairs that require urgent attention.
  • Estimated downtime for repairs.
  • Potential consequences if the issues are not addressed.
